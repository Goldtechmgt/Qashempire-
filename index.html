Watch Paid YouTube Videos
Earn by watching sponsored videos & ads

Get paid to watch selected YouTube videos. Typically each video pays a small amount — watch fully, follow any instructions (like liking or commenting) and mark it complete to receive the reward.

  • Tips: Watch at normal speed, complete any required actions exactly.
  • Payouts: Small per-video amounts; volume matters.
Watch Paid TikTok Videos
Short vertical videos that pay per view

Short-form content often pays quicker because videos are short. You may need to watch to a minimum watch time, engage, or follow creator pages to earn.

  • Tips: Let videos play fully and avoid skipping.
  • Good for: mobile-first users, short-sessions earning.
Answer Questions
Provide quick answers, quizzes, or surveys

Complete short quizzes, surveys or answer one-off questions. Payments vary by complexity — higher-paying tasks require more accurate or longer answers.

  • Tips: Read questions carefully; accuracy often determines payout.
  • Payouts: Higher for specialized or long-form responses.
Free Spinning (Daily)
Spin wheel to win coins, credits, or prizes

A daily or hourly spin gives a chance to win bonus credits, cash, or multiplier boosts. Best used as a way to compound small daily earnings into larger rewards.

  • Tips: Use daily to increase chances; watch for bonus days with higher rewards.
  • Good for: boosting earnings and keeping users returning.
Ad Clicking / Engagement
Click ads, view landing pages, or complete micro-tasks

Click-through tasks usually require you to visit an advertiser page and stay for a minimum time. These tasks pay small amounts but scale with volume.

  • Tips: Follow task rules exactly (min time, specific actions).
  • Security: Never provide personal or sensitive info while doing ad tasks.
Forex Trading (Advanced)
Trade currency pairs — higher risk, higher reward

Forex is a leveraged market where careful strategy, risk management, and education are essential. Only use funds you can afford to lose and learn with demo accounts first.

  • Tips: Start with demo trading, use stop-losses, and never trade with borrowed money.
  • Risk: High — require knowledge and caution.
Invite Friends (Referral)
Earn bonuses for successful referrals

Share your referral link and get rewards when friends sign up and complete qualifying actions. Referral bonuses can be a steady passive income when you have many active users.

  • Tips: Share in niche groups, explain how to get started to increase conversion.
  • Terms: Check qualifying actions; some invites only count after activity.
